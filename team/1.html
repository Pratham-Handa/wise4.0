Rajnickant Patel

Founder & Chairman

Mr. Rajnickant Patel is a distinguished leader in the financial services sector with an illustrious career spanning more than 4 decades. A visionary and strategist, Mr. Patel has held esteemed positions, including MD & CEO of the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) and the Indian Commodity Exchange (ICEX).

Among his landmark achievements is leading the corporatization of BSE, transforming it into a globally recognized institution with billion-dollar valuations. His profound experience includes the Reserve Bank of India, MNC banks, nationalized banks, capital markets, commodity markets, and corporate strategy, making him a luminary in the financial sector.

Mr. Patel’s governance acumen is evident in his advisory role to corporate boards and leading enterprises, where he fosters integrity with ethical practices and innovation to guide business leaders to success.

On the global landscape, he has represented India as Chairman of the South Asian Federation of Exchanges (SAFE), Director of the World Federation of Exchanges (WFE), and as a keynote speaker and thought leader, reinforcing his influence in shaping financial policies.

His awards and recognition include Brand Guardian of BSE as a Business Super Brand, Indira Super Achiever Award, Distinguished Fellow of the Institute of Directors (London), IT People Award for Innovation, and more.

With a legacy built on excellence, innovation, and strategic foresight, Mr. Rajnickant Patel continues to inspire and lead with unwavering commitment, driving progress and transformation across industry segments.
